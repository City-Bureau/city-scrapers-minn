Meetings and community events will be posted on the calendar below.  The Board formally meets twice a month - once at the Committee of the Board Meeting and once at the Board of Education Meeting.  Board of Education meetings are held at the Administration Building at 360 Colborne Street, unless otherwise noted.  If you need an interpreter at any of the listed Board Meetings (Hmong, Somali, Karen, Spanish, American Sign Language), please contact Sarah Dahlke (sarah.dahlke@spps.org) at least 2 weeks in advance.

Please view Public Notices page for more information, including special meetings that may be scheduled.
